HEAD

head - output the first part of files

# Output first 10 lines (default) of /etc/passwd:
$ head /etc/passwd


# -n = num lines
# Output first two lines of /etc/passwd:
$ head -n2 /etc/passwd

# -c = bytes. Output first 10 bytes:
$ head -c10 <file>

# Output multiple files:
head f1.txt f2.txt
==> f1.txt <==
a
b
c

==> f2.txt <==
b
c
d
