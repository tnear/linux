ASPELL

aspell - interactive spell checker

# -a = check individual words
$ aspell -a
@(#) calender  # misspelling of "calendar"
& calender 12 0: calendar, colander, ca lender, ca-lender, slender, Calder

-c = check file
$ aspell -c file.txt
# <follow the menu items>
# <'a', 'l', and 'i' are most commonly used>
