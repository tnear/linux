HD / HEXDUMP
hexdump - display file contents in hexadecimal, decimal, octal, or ascii

$ echo 'hello' > file.txt
$ hd file.txt

00000000  68 65 6c 6c 6f 0a         |hello.|
00000006


$ hexdump file.txt  # No right column
0000000 6568 6c6c 0a6f
0000006

$ hexdump -C file.txt  # Equivalent to 'hd'
00000000  68 65 6c 6c 6f 0a         |hello.|
00000006
