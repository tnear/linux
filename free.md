FREE

free - Display amount of free and used memory in the system

# Flags:
-h = human readable (shows units)

$ free -h
               total        used        free      shared  buff/cache   available
Mem:           7.8Gi       3.0Gi       2.5Gi       192Mi       2.2Gi       4.2Gi
Swap:          1.0Gi          0B       1.0Gi
