LOOK

look - display lines beginning with a given string

Note: 'grep' is typically a better choice.

# Find words in dictionary beginning with <STRING>
# When file name is left off, look searches /usr/share/dict/words
$ look metro

metro
metronome
metronomes
metropolis


# Search a particular file:
$ look "#include" Main.c

#include <stdio.h>
#include <string.h>
